Hi, I am Minzhi (Chloe)! 🍀

A Chinese-Korean Data Scientist based in Vancouver 🇨🇦

I am a graduate from UBC Master of Data Science in Computational Linguistics, with an undergraduate degree in Statistics from SFU.

After graduation, I joined Deepcoin as an AI Engineer, where I work on cutting edge AI solutions for cryptocurrency trading platforms. During my time at SFU, I gained valuable experience through three impactful internships:

  • Data Management & Analyst at Center for Heart Lung Innovation
  • Research Analyst at Surrey Schools
  • Research Assistant at Simon Fraser University

Each contributing to my expertise in data science, statistical analysis, and AI applications.
