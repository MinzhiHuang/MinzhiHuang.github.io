Who I Am

Hi, I'm Minzhi — Chloe.
A Chinese-Korean AI Engineer based in Vancouver.

I'm a graduate of the UBC Master of Data Science in Computational Linguistics, with an undergraduate degree in Statistics from SFU. My work sits at the intersection of language, data, and automation — building systems that are not just technically sound, but genuinely useful.

After graduation I joined Deepcoin as an AI Engineer, working on cutting-edge AI solutions for cryptocurrency trading platforms. I am also a Statistics & Coding Tutor (Part-time) at Look4, helping students navigate their data science journeys.

Currently, I am focused on building intelligent automation pipelines, exploring the latest developments in LLMs and agent frameworks, and shipping products that run while I sleep.
