BioLaySumm: Biomedical Lay Summarization

NLP • Large Language Models • Hackathon
🏆 Participated in BioLaySumm 2023 Shared Task @ ACL 2023 BioNLP Workshop. Benchmarked and optimized multiple large language models (BioMistral-7B, GPT-3.5, Mixtral-8x7B) to automatically generate lay summaries of complex biomedical research articles. The goal is to make scientific research accessible to non-expert audiences by transforming technical content into readable, comprehensible summaries with background information and simplified terminology.
